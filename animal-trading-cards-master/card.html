Whale

Whales feed their babies with their own milk, and they take extraordinarily good care of their young and teach them life skills

  • Scientific Name: Cetacea
  • Average Length: 98 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 50-100 years
  • Habitat: tropical or subtropical waters

Cetaceans have been extensively hunted for their meat, blubber and oil by commercial operations. They also face environmental hazards such as plastic buildup and ongoing climate change.
